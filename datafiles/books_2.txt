Elemental Strengths
Every creature has one or two elemental types. Every character has a single elemental type. Every spell has a single elemental type. There are 8 elemental types. Each with different strengths and weaknesses. A spell will deal more damage to a type it is strong against and less damage to a type it is weak against. Note that a strike attack during battle deals no elemental damage.
